# H065 Phase 0 Literature Check — TP-overlay on intraday channel-breakout

## Scope

H065 = H062 + ATR-scaled profit target (`TP = entry ± M × k_atr × ATR_n,t` at M ∈ {1.0, 1.5, 2.0, 2.5} R-multiples). Phase 0 lit-check covers (a) primary-source anchors for ATR-scaled TPs on Donchian / N-bar channel breakouts; (b) post-publication decay literature on the underlying intraday channel-breakout class (inherited from H062); (c) operational counter-evidence (TP overlay's expected effect on R-multiple distribution + skew).

## Primary anchors

### Underlying intraday channel-breakout class (inherited from H062 v1 lit-check verbatim)

H065 inherits H062's primary literature in full per the design.md §1.4 + §5 specification. The core anchors:

- [Faith, Curtis 2007. *Way of the Turtle: The Secret Methods That Turned Ordinary People into Legendary Traders*. McGraw-Hill. ISBN-13 978-0071486644](https://www.amazon.com/Way-Turtle-Secret-Methods-Successful/dp/0071486646) (*practitioner*). §4 Turtle System 1 (N=20 daily) and System 2 (N=55 daily) channel breakouts. **Critically for H065**: Turtle System 1 + 2 have NO explicit profit target; trades run until 2N ATR-trailing stop OR opposite-N-bar-channel-break exit. The TP-overlay is therefore a **departure** from the Faith 2007 canonical Turtle convention.
- [Wilder, J. Welles 1978. *New Concepts in Technical Trading Systems*. Trend Research](https://www.amazon.com/Concepts-Technical-Trading-Systems/dp/0894590278) (*practitioner*). Original ATR + ADX definitions.
- [Hong, Harrison; Stein, Jeremy C. 1999. "A Unified Theory of Underreaction, Momentum Trading, and Overreaction in Asset Markets." *Journal of Finance* 54(6):2143-2184, DOI 10.1111/0022-1082.00184](https://doi.org/10.1111/0022-1082.00184). The upstream causal mechanism for breakout-class strategies: gradual information diffusion → channel-break is the consensus-shifted signal.
- [Bouchaud, Jean-Philippe; Gefen, Yuval; Potters, Marc; Wyart, Matthieu 2004. "Fluctuations and Response in Financial Markets: The Subtle Nature of 'Random' Price Changes." *Quantitative Finance* 4(2):176-190, DOI 10.1080/14697680400000022](https://doi.org/10.1080/14697680400000022). Stop-order liquidity at multi-bar pivot levels amplifies the post-break move.

### TP-overlay literature (NEW for H065 vs H062)

- [Tharp, Van K. 1998. *Trade Your Way to Financial Freedom* (1st ed). McGraw-Hill. ISBN 978-0070647626](https://www.amazon.com/Trade-Your-Way-Financial-Freedom/dp/0071478716) (*practitioner*). §6 "R-multiple" framework. Tharp's canonical convention: trade-management at fixed risk:reward ratio (1:1, 1:2, 1:3, etc.) where ratio = ATR-scaled TP / ATR-scaled SL. **Critical for H065 v1**: Tharp argues the mean R-multiple (over many trades) is the load-bearing per-trade-edge unit; a strategy with mean-R > 0 is positive-expectancy. **The H065 inferential question is whether any M cell drives mean-R into strict-positive CI** (H062 v1 R-multiple-mean CI = [-0.017, +0.109] covered zero).
- [Vince, Ralph 1990. *Portfolio Management Formulas: Mathematical Trading Methods for the Futures, Options, and Stock Markets*. Wiley. ISBN 978-0471527565](https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Portfolio+Management+Formulas-p-9780471527565) (*practitioner*). Ch. 3 "Optimal f" — Kelly-criterion fraction-of-bankroll-at-risk per trade is bounded below 1/max(|R_loss|); the per-trade-R distribution determines the bankroll-survival probability per ADR-0017 §4.2.
- [López de Prado, Marcos 2018. *Advances in Financial Machine Learning*. Wiley. ISBN 978-1119482086](https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Advances+in+Financial+Machine+Learning-p-9781119482086) (*practitioner*). §3.5 "Triple-Barrier" labelling. The H065 TP+SL+EOD-flatten exit semantic is the AFML triple-barrier labeller's canonical signature (pt_sl[0] = M, pt_sl[1] = -1, vertical_barrier = EOD-flatten). H065 §4 explicitly maps onto this contract.

### Post-publication decay on intraday channel-breakout (inherited from H062 §1.4)

- [Marshall, Ben R.; Cahan, Rochester H.; Cahan, Jared M. 2008. "Can Commodity Futures Be Profitably Traded with Quantitative Market Timing Strategies?" *Journal of Banking & Finance* 32(9):1810-1819, DOI 10.1016/j.jbankfin.2007.12.011](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jbankfin.2007.12.011). 7846 trading rules including channel breakouts; no rule survives Romano-Wolf 2005 stepwise FWER correction on commodity futures 1984-2005.
- [Hsu, Po-Hsuan; Kuan, Chung-Ming 2005. "Reexamining the Profitability of Technical Analysis with Data Snooping Checks." *Journal of Financial Econometrics* 3(4):606-628, DOI 10.1093/jjfinec/nbi026](https://doi.org/10.1093/jjfinec/nbi026). **Verified finding** (per 2026-05-18 erratum at [`research/01_hypothesis_register/_erratum_hsu_kuan_2005_2026-05-18.md`](../_erratum_hsu_kuan_2005_2026-05-18.md)): profitable simple rules + complex strategies survive SPA correction in **young markets** (NASDAQ Composite + Russell 2000); **fail** in **mature markets** (DJIA + S&P 500). The distinction is **market maturity** (young vs mature), NOT market capitalization. NQ (Nasdaq-100) tracks NASDAQ Composite (SURVIVES category); ES (S&P 500) tracks S&P 500 (FAILS category). Prior wording stated "fail on large-cap Nasdaq" — empirically backwards.
- [Park, Cheol-Ho; Irwin, Scott H. 2007. "What Do We Know About the Profitability of Technical Analysis?" *Journal of Economic Surveys* 21(4):786-826, DOI 10.1111/j.1467-6419.2007.00519.x](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1467-6419.2007.00519.x). Meta-analysis; channel-breakout profitability concentrated pre-1990; weakening post-1990.

## Verdict

**`infrastructure-supported + TP-overlay is operational extension`**. The TP-overlay extension is a practitioner-canonical trade-management convention (Tharp 1998; the literature-canonical 1:1, 1:2, 1:3 risk:reward ratios). The underlying intraday channel-breakout is a partially-decayed factor per the H062 inherited literature; H065 v1 is structured as a **TP-overlay-on-partially-decayed-factor test**, NOT a strong-edge test.

**Critical caveat**: the TP-overlay departs from the Faith 2007 Turtle canonical convention (no profit target; let trades run to the trailing 2N stop OR opposite-channel break). H065 is therefore a **departure** from the Turtle convention, NOT a faithful reproduction. The empirical question is whether this departure produces measurable improvement on the survival-constrained primary metric vector per ADR-0017 §3.
