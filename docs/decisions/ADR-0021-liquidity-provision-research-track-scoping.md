# ADR-0021 — Liquidity-provision research track scoping

## Context

Every pre-registered SKIE hypothesis to date (H050 HMM regime-gated directional; H051 HMM-gated Kalman pairs; H052a HMM regime-gated first-hour ORB on futures; H052b HMM regime-gated QQQ 0DTE; H053 multi-timeframe regression with opening-bar mediation; H054 microstructure factor; H055 wick-rejection scalping with deterministic trend gate; H056-H059 successor tree per [ADR-0015](ADR-0015-component-stacking-master-architecture.md)) shares a structural posture: the strategy crosses the bid-ask spread on both entry and exit. The economic alpha source is **directional / regime / breakout / microstructure** signal; the cost model in [src/skie_ninja/backtest/costs/](../../src/skie_ninja/backtest/costs/) (`NT8EsNqRthV1CostModel`, `futures_orb_v1`) treats the spread as a friction. Every implementation submits **marketable** orders (`OnBarUpdate` issues a market or marketable-limit order that takes the resting side of the book), pays the half-spread × 2 + the CME-Globex taker fee, and earns whatever directional/regime alpha the signal generates.

This ADR scopes the categorically different mirror posture — **liquidity provision (price-making)**: the strategy posts **non-marketable limit orders** at or inside the touch, joins the resting queue on the CME-Globex price-time priority order book, and earns the bid-ask spread (plus any maker rebate where applicable) on each fill pair. The strategy is **paid** to provide immediacy to taker counterparties. The alpha source is spread capture; the dominant failure mode is **adverse selection** — the well-known [Glosten-Milgrom 1985](https://doi.org/10.1016/0304-405X(85)90044-3) result that an informed-trader population leaves the market-maker systematically picked off on the wrong side at every realized fill, so the *gross* spread captured must exceed the *adverse-selection cost* for the net P/L to be positive.

Four properties make this a categorically different track, not a within-track variant of existing SKIE hypotheses:

1. **Alpha source is structurally different.** Directional/regime hypotheses earn `E[return | signal]` from the underlying price process. Liquidity provision earns `E[spread - adverse_selection_cost | quote_state]` from the order-flow process. The two are mechanically orthogonal: a directional signal can be zero (no autocorrelation, no regime predictability) and a liquidity-provision strategy can still be net positive; a directional signal can be strong and a liquidity-provision strategy still net negative if the order-flow is informed-dominated. The ADR-0017 survival-constrained KPI vector (terminal-wealth-q05, Calmar-differential, profit-factor, R-multiple-mean) applies uniformly, but the *bench-mark* for liquidity provision is structurally different (see §Decision item 6 below).
2. **Failure mode is structurally different.** Directional-strategy realized failures are slippage + transaction cost + signal decay. Liquidity-provision realized failures are adverse selection ([Glosten-Milgrom 1985](https://doi.org/10.1016/0304-405X(85)90044-3); [Kyle 1985](https://doi.org/10.2307/1913210) λ as the canonical measure) + inventory risk ([Ho-Stoll 1981](https://doi.org/10.1016/0304-405X(81)90020-9); [Avellaneda-Stoikov 2008](https://doi.org/10.1080/14697680701381228) reservation-price formulation). Neither the existing [ADR-0017 §5](ADR-0017-survival-constrained-optimization-paradigm.md) kill-switch K-1..K-8 nor the existing cost model captures adverse-selection cost directly; new inferential primitive required (§Decision item 6 + follow-up `P1-ADVERSE-SELECTION-MEASUREMENT-PRIMITIVE`).
3. **Substrate is structurally different.** The existing post-Cell-I substrate at [data/processed/vendor_legacy_1min_roll_adjusted/](../../data/processed/vendor_legacy_1min_roll_adjusted/) is 1-min OHLCV bars. Liquidity-provision research requires **order-book-tier data** — CME-MDP3 ITCH-equivalent message-level book updates (Add/Modify/Cancel/Trade) with queue-position observability, per the canonical microstructure tradition ([Cont-Stoikov-Talreja 2010](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.1090.0780); [Cartea-Jaimungal-Penalva 2015](https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/algorithmic-and-highfrequency-trading/50CB23F0FA9B6F62A7E2622E991F0BCD)). 1-min bars are *insufficient by construction*: the bar mid-point hides the within-bar queue dynamics that determine whether a posted limit order would have filled and at what queue position.
4. **NinjaScript implementation pattern is structurally different.** Existing strategies use `OnBarUpdate` to issue marketable orders. Liquidity provision requires **`OnBarUpdate` → post non-marketable limit + `OnExecution` → manage fills + `OnOrderUpdate` → manage cancels and replaces + `OnMarketData` (level-1) or `OnMarketDepth` (level-2) → re-quote on book changes**. The existing kill-switch parameterization in [ADR-0017 §5](ADR-0017-survival-constrained-optimization-paradigm.md) (K-1..K-8) carries an interpretation-non-trivial conflict with K-3 ("no add to loser") because **inventory accumulation IS the business model** for a market-maker up to the inventory cap — K-3 needs a documented carve-out at the H100 pre-registration layer, not a project-level amendment.

This ADR is a **scoping ADR**, not an implementation ADR — analogous to [ADR-0006](ADR-0006-scope-extension-hmm-0dte.md) which scoped the HMM regime track and the 0DTE QQQ sibling repo as parallel tracks atop the existing directional posture. The substantive research deliverables (first pre-registered hypothesis, substrate ingest job, NinjaScript template, adverse-selection measurement primitive) are listed as follow-ups; the ADR commits the project to the track existing at the level of the hypothesis register and reserves the H100-series integer block, but does NOT commit to a launch date. The track is **deferred-but-pre-registered**: a future operator may activate it once the BLOCKING follow-ups (`P1-ORDERBOOK-INGEST-SCOPE-DESIGN`, `P1-CME-MDP3-LICENSE-NEGOTIATION`) land, without re-litigating the categorical-scope question.

## Decision

1. **Reserved integer block.** The hypothesis register reserves `H100`–`H149` for the liquidity-provision research track, structurally separate from the existing `H050`–`H059` directional/regime/breakout/microstructure block. The H100 series mirrors the H050 series convention (per-hypothesis subdirectory `research/01_hypothesis_register/H10X/` with `design.md` + `data_requirements.md` + `stage.md` + `failure_log.md` + versioned KPI report cards) and inherits all project-level standing rules ([ADR-0013](ADR-0013-permanent-exploration-no-archive-ninjascript-terminus.md) permanent exploration; [ADR-0014](ADR-0014-canonical-end-of-simulation-results-summary-tables.md) canonical 9-table summary; [ADR-0017](ADR-0017-survival-constrained-optimization-paradigm.md) survival-constrained KPI vector).

2. **First scheduled pre-registration: H100.** *Passive-limit-order quoting around the first-hour RTH opening range on CME ES/NQ futures*. Avellaneda-Stoikov 2008 reservation-price formulation with HJB-derived optimal half-spread `δ* = γ σ² (T − t) + (1/γ) ln(1 + γ/k)` ([Avellaneda-Stoikov 2008](https://doi.org/10.1080/14697680701381228) eq. 33 — to be primary-source-verified per [ADR-0014](ADR-0014-canonical-end-of-simulation-results-summary-tables.md) §F discipline at pre-registration time). Per-bar inventory hard cap of **1 contract** retail-tier, consistent with the [ADR-0001](ADR-0001-project-scope.md) capacity ceiling expressed as a delta-equivalent. H100 pre-registration is BLOCKED on `P1-ORDERBOOK-INGEST-SCOPE-DESIGN` landing — substrate must exist before the design.md `data_requirements.md` substrate dataset_checksum binding can be honored. Pre-registration also documents the [ADR-0017 §5](ADR-0017-survival-constrained-optimization-paradigm.md) K-3 carve-out (inventory accumulation up to the hard cap is the business model; the carve-out narrows K-3 to "no add-to-loser *beyond the inventory cap*" specifically for liquidity-provision hypotheses).

3. **Substrate extension required.** The existing 1-min OHLCV substrate at [data/processed/vendor_legacy_1min_roll_adjusted/](../../data/processed/vendor_legacy_1min_roll_adjusted/) is insufficient by construction (see §Context item 3). A new ingest job `vendor_legacy_orderbook` is reserved under [src/skie_ninja/data/ingest/](../../src/skie_ninja/data/ingest/); scope, message-schema, license terms, and storage layout (`data/processed/vendor_legacy_orderbook/`) are deferred to `P1-ORDERBOOK-INGEST-SCOPE-DESIGN` (BLOCKING-BEFORE-FIRST-H100-PRE-REG). The likely vendor is Databento with the CME-MDP3 itch feed (consistent with current Cell-I provenance); license + cost negotiation is an operator-action follow-up `P1-CME-MDP3-LICENSE-NEGOTIATION` (out-of-band). Storage tier sizing is non-trivial — CME-MDP3 raw message volumes on ES + NQ are approximately three orders of magnitude larger than 1-min OHLCV, so a partitioned-parquet layout with per-session compression and a queryable index will be required.

4. **NinjaScript implementation pattern.** A new per-strategy template `ninjascript/strategies/LiquidityProvision_template.cs` will land per follow-up `P1-NINJASCRIPT-LIQUIDITY-PROVISION-TEMPLATE`. The template enforces the canonical `OnBarUpdate` → `OnExecution` → `OnOrderUpdate` → `OnMarketData` event-loop structure, parameterizes the [Avellaneda-Stoikov 2008](https://doi.org/10.1080/14697680701381228) reservation-price + optimal-half-spread inputs, and binds the [ADR-0013 §5](ADR-0013-permanent-exploration-no-archive-ninjascript-terminus.md) NinjaScript-terminus discipline (every H100-series hypothesis MUST produce a working `.cs` implementation regardless of KPI report card values). The template is the C# analogue of the H055-v2-status H050 directional template at [ninjascript/strategies/](../../ninjascript/strategies/) and inherits the [ADR-0010](ADR-0010-multi-hour-run-process-protection.md) multi-hour-run process-protection contract for paper-trade sessions.

5. **HMM regime gating deferred to v2+.** Following the H055-v2-with-HMM precedent, H100 v1 is HMM-free; the [src/skie_ninja/models/regime/](../../src/skie_ninja/models/regime/) toolkit per [ADR-0005](ADR-0005-hmm-regime-toolkit.md) is NOT invoked at v1. Regime-conditioning of liquidity-provision strategies (intuition: HMM-identified high-volatility regimes should widen the quoted half-spread; HMM-identified mean-reverting regimes should tighten it) is reserved for an H100-v2 successor and tracked as a discrete future follow-up.

6. **Adverse-selection cost as load-bearing inferential primitive.** The dominant failure mode (§Context item 2) requires a measurable adverse-selection cost decomposition. The canonical microstructure literature provides three operational paths: (a) [Glosten-Milgrom 1985](https://doi.org/10.1016/0304-405X(85)90044-3) implied half-spread-decomposition from the conditional probability of an informed counterparty given fill direction; (b) [Kyle 1985](https://doi.org/10.2307/1913210) λ — slope of price impact on signed order flow — as a unidimensional adverse-selection measure; (c) [Hasbrouck 1991](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1540-6261.1991.tb03749.x) VAR-based decomposition of price changes into a permanent (information) component and a transient (inventory + microstructure noise) component, with the permanent-component magnitude as the adverse-selection cost. A new module under `src/skie_ninja/inference/adverse_selection.py` is reserved per follow-up `P1-ADVERSE-SELECTION-MEASUREMENT-PRIMITIVE`. The benchmark for H100-series Sharpe-vs-bench (the secondary KPI per [ADR-0017](ADR-0017-survival-constrained-optimization-paradigm.md) demotion and the existing ADR-0018 framing) is a **passive-spread-capture model** with the strategy quoting at the midpoint with no inventory management and no adverse-selection control — this is the canonical "naive market-maker" benchmark in the [Cartea-Jaimungal-Penalva 2015](https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/algorithmic-and-highfrequency-trading/50CB23F0FA9B6F62A7E2622E991F0BCD) treatment and is structurally analogous to the passive-long benchmark for directional hypotheses.

## Consequences

**Positive.**

- The project commits to a mirror alpha source with a categorically different failure mode, satisfying the [CLAUDE.md "Permanent-exploration framing"](../../CLAUDE.md) discipline that the program's purpose is aspirational innovation through exhaustive exploration.
- Reserved H100-series integer block prevents future hypothesis-ID collision (the H050-H099 directional/regime range is preserved cleanly; H100+ is unambiguously liquidity-provision).
- Pre-registering the K-3 carve-out at the ADR layer (the inventory-accumulation business model is the carve-out subject) prevents inline mid-flight design.md amendments to the [ADR-0017 §5](ADR-0017-survival-constrained-optimization-paradigm.md) kill-switch parameterization, which would otherwise compromise the [ADR-0013 §"Frozen pre-reg amendment"](ADR-0013-permanent-exploration-no-archive-ninjascript-terminus.md) discipline.

**Negative / Risk.**

- The `vendor_legacy_orderbook` ingest is substantively larger than the existing 1-min substrate. The storage tier, query-layer, and any downstream feature-engineering primitives are non-trivial to design and audit; the BLOCKING dependency cascade (substrate → first H100 pre-registration → first H100 production walk-forward → first H100 KPI report card → first H100 NinjaScript implementation) is multi-month at the current cycle cadence and is the load-bearing critical-path risk for the track.
- The CME-MDP3 license terms are not yet on file; cost negotiation is an operator-action follow-up. Worst-case unavailability would require a substrate-substitute decision (e.g., a per-session reconstructed-book from Databento minute-trades + Level-1 quotes) with documented degradation of inferential coverage.
- Adverse-selection measurement is the load-bearing primitive that the literature has not converged on (three competing operational paths per §Decision item 6); the primitive landing under `P1-ADVERSE-SELECTION-MEASUREMENT-PRIMITIVE` will require its own audit-remediate-loop trail per [SKILL.md](https://anthropic-skills.local/) discipline before the first H100 KPI report card can be emitted.

**Neutral.**

- The ADR commits no implementation, no launch date, and no specific architectural choice beyond the scoping items above. All material decisions (substrate schema, exact Avellaneda-Stoikov parameterization, K-3 carve-out wording, benchmark detail, NinjaScript event-loop contract) are deferred to the named follow-ups and will themselves go through audit-remediate-loop review at landing time.

**Follow-ups registered by this ADR**:

- `P1-ORDERBOOK-INGEST-SCOPE-DESIGN` — **BLOCKING-BEFORE-FIRST-H100-PRE-REG.** Design the `vendor_legacy_orderbook` ingest job (CME-MDP3 message schema, partitioned-parquet layout, queue-position observability primitive, Cycle-4 leak-canary discipline extension to message-level data).
- `P1-NINJASCRIPT-LIQUIDITY-PROVISION-TEMPLATE` — per-strategy `LiquidityProvision_template.cs` C# template with the canonical `OnBarUpdate` → `OnExecution` → `OnOrderUpdate` → `OnMarketData` event-loop structure.
- `P1-H100-PRE-REGISTRATION` — first liquidity-provision hypothesis pre-registration; gated on `P1-ORDERBOOK-INGEST-SCOPE-DESIGN`.
- `P1-ADVERSE-SELECTION-MEASUREMENT-PRIMITIVE` — `src/skie_ninja/inference/adverse_selection.py` module with at least one of the three canonical decompositions ([Glosten-Milgrom 1985](https://doi.org/10.1016/0304-405X(85)90044-3) / [Kyle 1985](https://doi.org/10.2307/1913210) λ / [Hasbrouck 1991](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1540-6261.1991.tb03749.x) VAR) primary-source-verified at implementation time.
- `P1-CME-MDP3-LICENSE-NEGOTIATION` — **operator-action, out-of-band.** License + cost negotiation with Databento (or substitute vendor) for CME-MDP3 message-level data; cost-of-information dossier to inform the substrate go/no-go decision.
- `P1-KYLE-LAMBDA-EMPIRICAL-CALIBRATION-ES-NQ` — empirical calibration of [Kyle 1985](https://doi.org/10.2307/1913210) λ on ES + NQ once the orderbook substrate lands; provides the unidimensional adverse-selection-cost reference value for the H100-v1 KPI report card.
- `P1-AVELLANEDA-STOIKOV-EQ-PIN-VERIFY` (verification-gap; non-blocking until H100 design.md freeze) — primary-source-pin the Avellaneda-Stoikov 2008 eq. 33 optimal-half-spread closed form against a publisher-side or author-copy PDF before any H100 design.md freezes the formula.
