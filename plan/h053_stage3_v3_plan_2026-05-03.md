**Closes**: F-2-1 (CPCV time-ordering violation; replaced entirely).

**Why 8 points not 16**: feasibility ceiling of 1122 sessions at the available IS train fold + EPV floor of 630 means the practical grid spans 630→1122 (≈1.78× range). At geometric ratio 1.0935 this gives 8 cells. A 16-point grid would have step ≈ 1.044, finer than the inner-CV window resolution (3-4 effective folds at n_train ≈ 800-1100 means adjacent cells are statistically indistinguishable).
