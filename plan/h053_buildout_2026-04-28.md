## Sequencing decision

Staged sequential over fully parallel because Cycles 8 → 9 → 10 form a probabilistic screen: each go/no-go materially changes the design surface of the next cycle. Cycle 8 (Stage-1 mediator-only) is the cheapest decisive evidence on whether the [Gao, Han, Li & Zhou 2018 *JFE*, doi:10.1016/j.jfineco.2018.05.009](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jfineco.2018.05.009) market-intraday-momentum effect persists in the 09:45-10:30 ET ES/NQ slice with project-grade infrastructure; a Sharpe-differential null at this stage makes Cycle 10's Stage-3 full-stack run unlikely to clear the §8 conjunctive-benchmark gate. Cycle 9 (Stage-2 multi-timeframe + mediation) only fires if Cycle 8 dominates HKS-reversal benchmark and beats climatological Brier. Cycle 10 enters the SPA family (3 ex-ante slots per design.md §8) and gates Cycle 11 — paper-trade scaffolding only fires on `archive(positive)`. Cycle 12 (Arm-3 LLM) is parallel-feasible only after Cycle 10 produces Arm-1+2 results that justify the Tier-3 spend; design.md §11.2 prereq 7 is binding (bit-identical-logit-tensor replay), so an early start on Cycle 12 risks throwing the work away if Arms 1+2 archive null. Merging concurrent edits across [src/skie_ninja/features/h053/](../src/skie_ninja/features/h053/) + [src/skie_ninja/inference/mediation/](../src/skie_ninja/inference/mediation/) + [src/skie_ninja/models/llm_arm/](../src/skie_ninja/models/llm_arm/) would also cost more in review than the ~3-day parallel-save.

## Completion criteria per cycle

A cycle is `done` iff all of:

1. All new modules green on `pytest -m "not integration" -n auto`.
2. `quant-auditor` returns zero HIGH or CRITICAL findings; MEDIUM findings either remediated or tracked as follow-ups in [docs/audits/audit_trail_2026-04-28_h053-design.md](../docs/audits/audit_trail_2026-04-28_h053-design.md) Open follow-up tags table.
3. `reproducibility-verifier` confirms pinned deps, seeds, checksums, env capture (where applicable), and — for Cycles 10 + 12 — bit-identical replay across two consecutive runs on pinned hardware per design.md §11.3.
4. Dated audit trail committed to [docs/audits/](../docs/audits/) with format `audit_trail_{YYYY-MM-DD}_cycle{N}-h053-{slug}.md`.
5. [README.md](../README.md) + [CLAUDE.md](../CLAUDE.md) §Implemented-infrastructure section updated; this plan's §Cycle status flipped to `[x]` with the audit-trail link.
6. Conventional-commit on a single logical change; git HEAD captured in the cycle's audit trail.

## Post-Cycle-12 trajectory

After Cycle 11 produces 60 session-days of paper-trade results (or Cycle 12 lands an LLM-arm `archive(positive)`):

- **Live promotion** gated by [CLAUDE.md](../CLAUDE.md) §Execution bar — realized paper-trade Sharpe within Lo 2002 / Opdyke 2007 / Ledoit-Wolf 2008 CI of backtested Sharpe across the 60-session-day floor; `P1-H053-KDD-EMPIRICAL-CALIBRATION` follow-up closed before live; kill-switch active.
- **SPA family update.** H050 / H051 / H052a / H052b / H053 share the same `universe_snapshot_id` per design.md §8 + §12; H053's three slots (Arm 1, Arm 2, Arm 3) are ex-ante-fixed and survive prerequisite failure as `archive(null, prerequisite-not-met)` per design.md §8.
- **Feature-factory inheritance.** Future hypotheses (H054, H055, …) inherit the Cycle 7 feature factory (Blocks A/B/C/D + archetype classifier) and the Cycle 9 mediation estimator at near-zero marginal cost.
- **Paper-trade floor.** 60 session-days per [CLAUDE.md](../CLAUDE.md) §Execution bar — immovable.
